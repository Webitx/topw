What is Microfiber

  • A kind of man-made fiber contains polyamide and polyester.
  • Polyamide: the absorbing and quick drying fiber.
  • Polyester: the scrubbing and cleaning fiber.
  • Each square inch in a microfiber product contains approx. 90,000 microfibers.
  • Hold up to 7 times than its weight in dirt, oil and liquid.

Microfiber is light weight, super water absorption, lint free, eco-friendly and at the same time, economic. Less cost than cotton but more efficient. The ideal material for cleaning、bath and outdoor.
